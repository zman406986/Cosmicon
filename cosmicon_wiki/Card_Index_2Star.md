## Strategy Notes / 策略要点

### Chimera / 奇美拉

- **Role**: Burst attacker / 爆发型攻击者
- Rewards matching numbers on attack dice. A pair gives +3, but specifically rolling 4s gives +7 per match - significantly stronger.
- Best with high-count low-value dice to maximize pair probability.
- **定位**: 爆发型攻击者，相同点数越多攻击加成越高。
- 掷出4点时加成翻倍以上，是核心爆发手段。

### Dromas / 大地兽

- **Role**: DoT attacker / 持续伤害型攻击者
- Applies 2 Poison stacks per attack when all dice are even. Poison deals damage over time and decays.
- Stacks infinitely - longer games mean more Poison pressure on the opponent.
- **定位**: 持续伤害型，偶数骰全中时叠加中毒层数。
- 多回合累积中毒可造成大量持续伤害。

### Automaton Beetle / 自动机兵·甲虫

- **Role**: Defensive tank / 防御型坦克
- Reroll on defense, plus Forcefield (immune to general attacks) and 8 Strength for next turn if dice have 3 consecutive numbers.
- Low HP (10) but high DEF Level (3) - relies on Forcefield to survive.
- **定位**: 防御型坦克，力场免疫常规攻击，力量层数反哺下回合输出。
- HP极低但防御等级高，依赖力场生存。

### Trashcan / 垃圾桶

- **Role**: Scaling attacker / 成长型攻击者
- Gains 2 Strength after each attack (4 if all dice are even). The even-dice bonus rewards high-count low-value dice setups.
- Strength stacks accumulate over multiple turns, making this card stronger in longer games.
- **定位**: 持续成长的攻击者，偶数骰加成鼓励使用大量低面骰子。
- 多回合累积力量层数，后期输出可观。

### Furbo Journalist / 狸猫记者

- **Role**: Reactive defender / 反击型防御者
- Deals 2 Instant Damage when hit during defense (4 if all dice are odd). Forces opponents to choose between attacking and taking counter-damage.
- The odd-dice bonus incentivizes specific die configurations.
- **定位**: 反击型防御者，对手攻击时会受到瞬伤反噬。
- 全奇数骰子配置可将反击伤害翻倍。

### Banacademic Office's Fortune BananAdvisor / 惊梦剧团的圆幕先生

- **Role**: Sustain defender / 续航型防御者
- Gains a reroll on defense, heals 5 HP if no damage is taken, and can permanently increase Defense Level once if HP drops to 5 or below.
- Excellent for stalling strategies and surviving burst damage.
- **定位**: 续航型防御者，重投+回血+永久防御提升三重保障。
- 低血量时触发防御等级永久提升，适合持久战。

### Senior Staff: Team Leader / 资深员工·组长

- **Role**: Versatile all-rounder / 全能型
- Gains +1 Attack for each unique number when attacking, and +1 Defense for each unique number when defending.
- Rewards diverse die pools (many different numbers) rather than matching sets.
- **定位**: 攻守兼备的全能型，鼓励使用多样化骰子组合。
- 骰子点数越丰富，攻防加成越高。
